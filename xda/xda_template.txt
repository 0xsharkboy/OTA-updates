[SIZE="3"]- [COLOR="red"]Have OOS 10.3 flashed to both slots!![/COLOR]

- Boot into recovery
- Flash  BUILD.zip
- Flash TWRP.zip
- Wipe Cache
- Format data
- Reboot to recovery (optional)

[SIZE=5][B]PLEASE DO NOT FLASH GAPPS, THEY ARE ALREADY INCLUDED

First Time Install / Clean Flash from MIUI[/B][/SIZE]

Backup your entire internal storage as it will be wiped in the final step before rebooting to the system.

Flash [URL='https://xiaomifirmwareupdater.com/archive/firmware/marble/']MIUI 14 firmware[/URL] of your region if coming from HyperOS

Flash the linked [URL='https://t.me/EvolutionXMarble/1/128558']recovery[/URL] image by performing the following:

[CODE]fastboot flash recovery filename.img[/CODE]

After finishing with that, get into recovery by pressing the power button and volume up button. Or run "fastboot reboot recovery"

After that, Install the ROM either by doing the normal flashing step or use adb sideload.

Then kindly perform a format data in the recovery afterwards.

Reboot! Enjoy! #KeepEvolving
